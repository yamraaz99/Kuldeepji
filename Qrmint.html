Frequently Asked Questions

How does QR Mint ensure QR codes are scannable?

QR Mint uses industry-standard error correction and automatically adjusts the code complexity based on content. When adding logos or customizations, the system ensures the QR code maintains proper contrast and structure to remain scannable by all standard QR code readers.

Can I add my logo to the QR code?

Yes, you can upload and add your logo to the center of any QR code. The system automatically adjusts error correction levels to ensure your QR code remains scannable even with a logo added. You can also adjust the size and position of the logo for optimal results.

What file formats can I download my QR code in?

QR Mint supports downloading your QR codes in PNG (with transparency), JPG (high-quality), and PDF (vector format) formats. PNG is recommended for digital use where transparency is needed, JPG for web and social media, and PDF for high-quality print materials.

Do I need to create an account to use QR Mint?

No, QR Mint provides full functionality without requiring account creation or registration. You can generate and customize QR codes instantly. However, creating an optional account allows you to save your QR codes for future access and editing.

Are there any limits to how many QR codes I can generate?

QR Mint offers unlimited QR code generation with no restrictions on the number of codes you can create. This makes it ideal for businesses and individuals who need multiple QR codes for different purposes.

Can I edit my QR code after creating it?

Yes, QR Mint allows you to edit all aspects of your QR code at any time before downloading. You can modify the content, change colors, add or remove logos, adjust size settings, and preview the changes in real-time before finalizing your design.
