Frequently Asked Questions

Is AI Bio Generator free to use?

Yes, AI Bio Generator is completely free to use with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or usage limitations. You can generate as many bios as you need without creating an account.

Does AI Bio Generator store my personal data?

No, AI Bio Generator does not store any personal information or the bios you generate. The tool processes your inputs purely on the client-side and doesn't save any data after your session ends, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I use the generated bios for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! You can use the generated bios for any purpose, including personal and commercial social media accounts, business pages, and professional profiles.

How accurate are the character counts for different platforms?

The tool strictly adheres to the official character limits for each platform (Instagram: 150 characters, Twitter/X: 160 characters, LinkedIn: 220 characters for headline, Threads: similar to Instagram), ensuring that your bio won't be truncated when posted.

Can I edit the generated bios?

While the tool doesn't include an editor, you can easily copy the generated bio to your clipboard and paste it into any text editor for further customization before using it on your social profiles.

How often is AI Bio Generator updated?

The tool is regularly updated to maintain compatibility with platform changes, improve AI generation quality, and add new features. Check back often for the best experience.
