Installation Guide

How to Install the Amazing Dealz App

1

Download the APK

Visit https://amazingdealz.netlify.app/App on your Android device and tap the download button.

Note: Make sure you're using a reliable browser like Chrome or Firefox.

2

Enable Unknown Sources

Before installation, you need to allow your device to install apps from unknown sources:

  1. Go to Settings on your device
  2. Navigate to Security or Privacy
  3. Enable Unknown Sources or Install Unknown Apps
  4. Select the browser you downloaded the APK with and toggle Allow from this source

Note: This step may vary slightly depending on your Android version and device manufacturer.

3

Install the App

Locate the downloaded APK file in your Downloads folder or notification panel and tap on it to start installation.

Review the permissions and tap Install to proceed.

4

Open and Enjoy

Once installation is complete, tap Open to launch the Amazing Dealz app. You can now start browsing for the best deals!

For security reasons, you may want to disable the Unknown Sources setting after installation.

Usage Instructions

Navigating the App

  • Home Screen: View all current deals sorted by recency. Pull down to refresh for the latest updates.
  • Filter Bar: Use the filter options at the top to view deals from specific stores like Amazon, Flipkart, or Myntra.
  • Deal Cards: Tap on any deal card to see full details and visit the store to purchase.
  • Refresh: Use the refresh button in the top right to manually update the deals list.

Accessing Deal Categories

  • Store Filters: Tap on Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, or Other to see store-specific deals.
  • All Deals: Select "All" to view deals from every store in one feed.
  • Deal Status: Each deal shows current status (Active or Expired) and when it was last updated.
  • Time Filter: The app automatically shows deals from the past 15 hours by default.

Tips for Maximizing Savings

Enable Notifications: Don't miss out on limited-time deals by keeping app notifications enabled.
Check Regularly: The best deals often sell out quickly, so check the app multiple times daily.
Act Fast: When you see a great deal, don't hesitate—they can expire or sell out rapidly.
Compare Prices: Even with deals, compare across different stores for the best possible price.
Check Deal Time: Look at when the deal was posted to gauge how long it might remain available.
Use Refresh: The refresh button ensures you're seeing the most current available deals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Amazing Dealz app free to use?

Yes, the Amazing Dealz app is completely free to download and use. We don't charge any fees or have any in-app purchases. Our goal is to help you find the best deals without any cost to you!

Is it safe to install an APK file from your website?

Yes, our APK is completely safe to install. We regularly scan it for malware and viruses to ensure security. The app only requires minimal permissions to function and doesn't collect unnecessary personal data. However, as with any APK installation, make sure you're downloading directly from our official website at amazingdealz.netlify.app/App.

How often are new deals added to the app?

We update the app with new deals multiple times throughout the day. The app shows deals from the past 15 hours by default, and you can manually refresh anytime to see the latest additions. Our team works continuously to find and add the best deals across all supported stores.

Why does the app need permission to install from unknown sources?

Since our app isn't distributed through the Google Play Store, Android requires this permission for security reasons. This is standard for any app installed via APK file. You can disable the "unknown sources" permission after installation for added security. We're working on adding the app to official app stores in the future.

How do I report a deal that's no longer available?

If you find a deal that's expired or no longer available, you can report it through the contact form in the app or on our website. Please include the deal name and when you found it unavailable. We appreciate users helping us maintain accurate information!

Will there be an iOS version of the app?

We're currently focusing on our Android app, but an iOS version is in our development roadmap. If you're an iOS user, you can still access all our deals through our mobile-friendly website at amazingdealslive.netlify.app. We'll announce on our website when the iOS app becomes available.
