How to Use

Paste Your URL

Copy your long product or deal URL from any shopping website and paste it into the input field on our homepage.

(Optional) Add a Custom Slug

Want to personalize your link? Enter your preferred custom ending in the dedicated field. For example: "amazingdealz.netlify.app/summer-sale" instead of random characters.

Click "Shorten" and Share!

Hit the "Shorten" button and your new, clean URL is ready! Use the copy button to instantly copy it to your clipboard for sharing.

Why We Built This

Amazing Dealz URL Shortener was born from a real need in the deal-sharing community. As Telegram channel operators ourselves, we faced several challenges:

  • Product URLs from shopping sites are extremely long and unattractive
  • Long URLs break the flow of messages and look unprofessional
  • Standard URL shorteners don't offer customization options for deal descriptors
  • We needed something that matched our deal-focused branding

Our tool is specifically designed for deal hunters who want to quickly share discounts, offers, and special finds with their audience on Telegram channels, WhatsApp groups, or social media platforms.
